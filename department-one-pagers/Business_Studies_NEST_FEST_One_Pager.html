Real-World Application = Better Learning Outcomes

Research shows students retain concepts 70% better when they see real-world applications.

NEST-FEST provides experiential learning that reinforces classroom instruction:

  • See how entrepreneurs use SWOT analysis, Porter's Five Forces, and business model canvas
  • Observe professional business presentations and Q&A with judges
  • Network with Austin's business community and startup founders
  • Gain inspiration for career paths beyond traditional corporate roles
