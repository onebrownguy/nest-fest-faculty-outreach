Dear Professor Cepeda,

I hope this message finds you well! As a Computer Science faculty member, you're teaching technical skills that power countless Austin startups - and NEST-FEST on November 7th connects your students with that entrepreneurial ecosystem.

NEST-FEST is a student-led entrepreneurship pitch event supported by Austin Community College's Office of the Future under the newly formed Entrepreneurship & Innovation Academy. **WHY THIS MATTERS FOR CS/IT STUDENTS:**

- **Tech Entrepreneurship:** See software and apps pitched as viable businesses
- **Product Development:** How developers identify problems worth solving
- **Austin Startup Scene:** Connect with one of the fastest-growing tech hubs in the U.S.
- **Career Alternatives:** Founding or joining startups vs. corporate tech roles
