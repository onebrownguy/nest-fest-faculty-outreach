**COMMUNICATION IN ENTREPRENEURSHIP:**
- **Elevator Pitches:** Condensing complex ideas into compelling 3-5 minute narratives
- **Value Propositions:** Clear messaging about what makes a business unique
- **Story Arcs:** Problem ‚Üí Solution ‚Üí Impact narrative structure
- **Audience Engagement:** Reading the room and adapting messaging in real-time

**The Reality:** Entrepreneurs live or die by their ability to communicate value.

**OPTIONAL EXTRA CREDIT FRAMEWORK:**

For students who attend NEST-FEST:

**Rhetorical Analysis of Pitches (3-4 pages):**
1. **Select 3 Pitches:** Choose three different presentations
2. **Narrative Analysis:** Evaluate storytelling structure and message organization
3. **Rhetorical Strategies:** Analyze persuasive techniques and appeals
4. **Communication Effectiveness:** Assess clarity, engagement, and impact
5. **Comparative Evaluation:** Which pitch communicated most effectively and why?

**Critical Questions:**
- How did entrepreneurs structure their narratives (problem-solution-impact)?
- What rhetorical devices enhanced persuasiveness?
- How clear was the value proposition and messaging?
- How effective was audience engagement and adaptation?
