I hope this message finds you well! As an Accounting professor at ACC, you teach the financial discipline that determines startup success or failure - and NEST-FEST on November 7th demonstrates exactly why accounting knowledge is critical for entrepreneurs.

NEST-FEST is a student-led entrepreneurship pitch event supported by Austin Community College's Office of the Future under the newly formed Entrepreneurship & Innovation Academy. Your students will see why accounting is the foundation of every viable business.

**WHY THIS MATTERS FOR ACCOUNTING STUDENTS:**

- **Financial Projections:** Revenue forecasts, expense budgets, profit margins
- **Cash Flow Management:** Burn rate, runway, break-even analysis
- **Unit Economics:** Cost per customer, lifetime value calculations
- **Business Viability:** Which pitches have realistic financials vs. wishful thinking
