**Tech Product Analysis (3-4 pages):**
1. **Select 3 Tech Pitches:** Choose software/web product pitches
2. **Technical Evaluation:** Assess technical feasibility and implementation
3. **Product-Market Fit:** Evaluate problem identification and solution design
4. **MVP Strategy:** Analyze minimum viable product approach
5. **Recommendations:** What technical improvements would strengthen the business?

**Critical Questions:**
- Is the technical solution feasible given current technology?
- Does the product solve a real problem worth solving?
- What MVP approach would validate the idea most efficiently?
- What technical risks or challenges does this business face?

**CAREER PATHWAYS FOR SOFTWARE DEVELOPERS:**

NEST-FEST demonstrates career alternatives to traditional dev roles:
- **Technical Founder:** Build and launch your own software product
- **Startup Developer:** Join early-stage companies and shape product development
- **Technical Co-Founder:** Partner with non-technical founders
- **Freelance Developer:** Build products for multiple clients/startups
- **Developer Entrepreneur:** Turn coding skills into business ownership
