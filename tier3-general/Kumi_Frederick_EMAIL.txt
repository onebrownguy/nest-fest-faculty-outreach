I hope this message finds you well! As Computer Science faculty at ACC, you're teaching programming and technical skills that drive innovation. NEST-FEST on November 7th shows your students how to turn code into business opportunities.

**WHY THIS MATTERS FOR CS STUDENTS:**

Computer science skills are the foundation of modern entrepreneurship:

**CS IN ENTREPRENEURSHIP:**
- **Software Products:** Building apps, platforms, and digital solutions
- **Technical Problem-Solving:** Identifying problems that code can solve
- **MVP Development:** Rapid prototyping and product validation
- **Austin Tech Ecosystem:** Connecting with one of America's top tech startup hubs
- **Technical Founders:** CS students make highly sought-after startup co-founders
