I hope this message finds you well! As Engineering Technology faculty at ACC, you're teaching product development and manufacturing skills that create entrepreneurial opportunities. NEST-FEST on November 7th showcases maker entrepreneurship and hardware startups.

NEST-FEST is a student-led entrepreneurship pitch event supported by Austin Community College's Office of the Future under the newly formed Entrepreneurship & Innovation Academy. Led by Program Director Harshal Shah and Faculty Supervisor Dr. Andrea Kehoe, this event brings together innovative ideas from ACC students with mentorship and recognition to drive the next wave of student entrepreneurs.

**WHY THIS MATTERS FOR ENGINEERING TECH STUDENTS:**

Engineering skills create product entrepreneurship opportunities:

**ENGINEERING IN ENTREPRENEURSHIP:**
- **Product Development:** Prototyping, manufacturing, bringing ideas to market
- **Maker Entrepreneurship:** Hardware startups and physical product businesses
- **Manufacturing Strategy:** Production scaling and supply chain management
- **Technical Innovation:** Engineering solutions to real-world problems
- **Austin Ecosystem:** Growing hardware startup scene (Tesla, Applied Materials, etc.)
