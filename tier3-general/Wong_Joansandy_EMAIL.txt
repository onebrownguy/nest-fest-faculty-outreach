I hope this message finds you well! As Communication Studies faculty at ACC, you know that interpersonal communication skills build relationships that drive business success. NEST-FEST on November 7th creates networking opportunities where communication skills create connections.

**WHY THIS MATTERS FOR COMMUNICATION STUDENTS:**

Interpersonal communication is how entrepreneurs build the relationships that create opportunities:

**COMMUNICATION IN ENTREPRENEURSHIP:**
- **Networking Skills:** Building professional relationships with investors, mentors, customers
- **Active Listening:** Understanding stakeholder needs and concerns
- **Relationship Building:** Creating trust and rapport with business partners
- **Conflict Resolution:** Managing disagreements and finding common ground
- **Collaborative Communication:** Working effectively in teams and partnerships

**The Reality:** Business success depends on relationship quality, which depends on communication skills.
