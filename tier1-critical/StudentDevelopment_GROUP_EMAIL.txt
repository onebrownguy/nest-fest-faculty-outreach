Dear Student Development Faculty,

I hope this message finds you well! I'm reaching out to your team because NEST-FEST on November 7th aligns perfectly with Student Development's mission - helping students build transferable skills that lead to success in ANY career path.

NEST-FEST is a student-led entrepreneurship pitch event supported by Austin Community College's Office of the Future under the newly formed Entrepreneurship & Innovation Academy. Led by Program Director Harshal Shah and Faculty Supervisor Dr. Andrea Kehoe, this event brings together innovative ideas from ACC students with mentorship and recognition to drive the next wave of student entrepreneurs.

**WHY THIS MATTERS FOR ALL ACC STUDENTS:**

You don't have to be a business major to benefit from entrepreneurial thinking. The skills students develop by attending NEST-FEST apply to every career:

**TRANSFERABLE SKILLS DEVELOPED:**
- **Communication:** Pitching ideas clearly and persuasively (valuable in job interviews, project presentations, any career)
- **Critical Thinking:** Evaluating business problems and solutions (applies to any field requiring problem-solving)
- **Adaptability:** Understanding how entrepreneurs pivot and adjust (essential in rapidly changing job markets)
- **Confidence:** Watching peers pitch ideas builds courage to share your own thoughts in professional settings
- **Growth Mindset:** Entrepreneurship teaches resilience, learning from failure, and continuous improvement

**Student Development Connection:**
- These are the exact skills Student Development programs help students build
- NEST-FEST provides experiential learning outside traditional classroom settings
- Students see peers taking initiative and pursuing ambitious goals
- Event demonstrates ACC's commitment to holistic student development
